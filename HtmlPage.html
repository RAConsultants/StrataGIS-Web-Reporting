Water is essential to growth, to health, and to the very existence of every city and community, large or small. RA visualizes water engineering as a balancing act. We insist water engineering requires both technical expertise and the consideration of surrounding communities. While Federal Regulation controls much of the water industry today, RA ensures providing satisfactory water service in an economically efficient and technically sufficient manner.

RA Professionals have experience in long distance, large diameter transmission mains, facility planning & design, as well as construction inspection and field activities. RA's professionals are especially experienced in the key aspects of network analysis, water distribution system layout and design. RA understands that our water clients must serve the demands of their growing user bases; therefore, we continue to upgrade to state-of-the-art water technology and maintain systems' pristine condition.

Professionals of RA work diligently to stay abreast of the ever-changing design regulations. Most importantly, RA professionals always work with the needs of the owner, the constructor and the community in mind
